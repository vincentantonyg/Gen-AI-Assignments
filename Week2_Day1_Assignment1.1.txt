## 2. Introduction

This document outlines the test strategy for the enhancement of **Guidewire PolicyCenter** for the **Commercial Auto Line of Business (LOB)**. The purpose is to define the testing approach, scope, methodologies, environments, and deliverables to ensure high-quality releases.

---

## 3. Objectives

- Validate enhancements in Commercial Auto workflows
- Ensure seamless integration with external systems
- Minimize production defects through structured testing
- Support Agile/Scrum delivery with monthly releases
- Ensure business continuity and regulatory compliance

---

## 4. Scope of Testing

### In Scope:
- Enhancements in Commercial Auto Policy lifecycle:
  - Submission
  - Quoting
  - Binding
  - Policy Issuance
  - Endorsements
  - Renewals
- Integration validation with:
  - Rating Engines (3rd Party)
  - Document Management Systems
  - SAP Billing System

### Out of Scope:
- New LOB implementation
- Legacy system full regression (outside impacted areas)

---

## 5. Testing Approach

### 5.1 Test Levels

#### Functional Testing
- Validate business rules, UI workflows, and policy transactions
- Coverage of all enhancement-related changes

#### Integration Testing
- Validate data exchange between PolicyCenter and:
  - Rating Engine
  - Document Management
  - SAP Billing

#### System Testing
- End-to-end validation of complete policy lifecycle
- Cross-system validation

#### User Acceptance Testing (UAT)
- Business validation of enhancements
- Conducted by business stakeholders

#### Regression Testing
- Manual regression of impacted modules
- Focus on critical business flows

---

## 6. Test Methodology

- Testing follows **Agile/Scrum model**
- Test activities aligned with sprint cycles
- Continuous collaboration with Dev, BA, and Product Owners
- Test case design based on:
  - User Stories
  - Acceptance Criteria
  - Business Rules

---

| Environment | Purpose                     |

## 8. Test Data Strategy

- Combination of:
  - Synthetic test data
  - Masked production data (if applicable)
- Data prepared for:

  - Integration scenarios
- Data versioning maintained for repeatability

---

## 9. Test Execution Strategy

### Step-by-Step Implementation:

1. **Requirement Analysis**
   - Analyze user stories and acceptance criteria
   - Identify impacted modules

2. **Test Planning**
   - Define scope, timelines, resources
   - Risk identification

3. **Test Design**
   - Create test scenarios and test cases
   - Review with stakeholders

4. **Test Environment Setup**
   - Validate QA and SIT readiness
   - Integration endpoints validation

5. **Test Data Preparation**
   - Create and validate required datasets

6. **Test Execution**
   - Execute Functional tests in QA
   - Execute Integration tests
   - Execute System tests in SIT

7. **Defect Management**
   - Log defects with severity and priority
   - Track lifecycle until closure

8. **Regression Testing**
   - Execute regression suite for impacted areas

9. **UAT Support**
   - Support business users during UAT
   - Fix validation and re-testing

10. **Test Closure**
   - Prepare test summary report
   - Lessons learned and sign-off

---

## 10. Defect Management

- Defects logged in defect tracking tool (e.g., JIRA)
- Severity Levels:
  - Critical
  - High
  - Medium
  - Low
- Daily defect triage meetings
- SLA-based resolution tracking

---

## 11. Entry and Exit Criteria

### Entry Criteria:
- Requirements are signed off
- Test environment is ready
- Test data is available

### Exit Criteria:
- All planned test cases executed
- Critical and High defects closed
- UAT sign-off received
- Test summary report completed

---

## 12. Risks and Mitigation

| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|-----------|
| Integration system downtime | Use stubs/mocks where possible |
| Incomplete requirements | Early BA involvement |
| Environment instability | Dedicated environment support |
| Data issues | Early data preparation and validation |
